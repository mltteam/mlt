The Story Behind

Our water hyacinth bag collection is inspired by the natural beauty of Vietnam's rivers. Lục bình (water hyacinth) is an invasive plant that local artisans transform into stunning fashion pieces, preventing ecological damage while empowering their communities.

With a spacious interior and sturdy handles, this bag is perfect for beach days, market trips, or as a statement accessory. Each bag carries a story of sustainability, creativity, and cultural heritage.

Our Eco Commitment

Turning invasive water hyacinth into stunning accessories helps restore our waterways while reducing waste. By choosing our bag, you support environmental stewardship and empower local artisans.

  • Repurposes invasive plants into beautiful fashion
  • Promotes upcycling and minimal waste
  • Supports artisan communities and fair trade

Frequently Asked Questions

Is the bag water resistant? +

While the bag can handle light moisture, it is not fully waterproof. Avoid heavy rain or submerging it in water.

How do I clean the bag? +

Use a soft brush or damp cloth to gently remove dust. Do not use strong chemicals or bleach.

Are the materials sustainably sourced? +

Yes! Our water hyacinth is harvested from invasive growth in local waterways.
